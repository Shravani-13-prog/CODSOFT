About

Welcome to Coral Shades, the profile of a self-taught artist who loves to create cute and funky art. I'm passionate about using vibrant colors and patterns to bring life to my designs. Ever since I was a child, I've been fascinated by colours and the endless creations that can be done with them! This account shows my journey in the world of art.

Art Tips

    Here are some art tips that'll be helpful if you are a beginner.
  • Experiment with different art styles and media.
  • Always sketch lightly before going bold with color.
  • Connect with other artists. You may get valuable advices.
  • Use mood boards and color palette references online to make your art look cooler.
  • Look up for inspiration on Pinterest!.
